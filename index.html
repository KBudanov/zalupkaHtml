GAYTEAM

Jordan Godwin

Hailing from Cardiff, UK, Jordan Godwin has quickly made a name for himself in the BMX world. He placed 4th in XG California 2023 Real BMX and 9th in his rookie appearance at XG 2022 Street. Jordan claimed 1st at the May 2024 FISE event in Montpellier, France, and 3rd at the September 2023 Simple Session in Tallinn, Estonia. Named the 2023 Nora Cup Street Rider of the Year, Jordan is also a creative force and co-owns the clothing brand Doomed. He enjoys drawing, filming and editing. His favorite X Games moment is any run by Garrett Reynolds.
