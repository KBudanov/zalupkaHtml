ABOUT 4:20

"4.20" isn’t just a BMX shop—it’s a community of true street riders. We don’t chase trends—we create them. The streets, parks, and dirt jumps are our home, and we know exactly what you need because we ride every day ourselves.

Here, you’ll find real hardcore—frames, wheelsets, parts, and merch from top brands and local craftsmen. We support the culture, our own, and everyone ready to live on the pedals. Drop by, connect, and grab what will make your setup unstoppable.

Welcome to 4.20—where every day feels like the last trick before sunset.

OUR RIDERS

ANDREW NESTEROV

Andrew Nesterov is a top-tier BMX street rider from Odessa, Ukraine, known for his insane switch tailwhips. His technical skills and creative approach make him one of the most exciting riders in the scene. Competing at Simple Session, he climbed from 37th place in 2023 to 16th in 2024, proving his rapid progression. His “VOLYA” edit showcased groundbreaking tricks, earning him the Best Rider of the Year title at Street Combat Pro 2024. Sponsored by Street Culture Magazine, SSUR, OtherHood, and JunkRide, Nesterov keeps pushing BMX to the next level. Check out his riding in: VOLYA – DIG LOCALS.

NOTRIK/DZUSOV_G

@dzusov_g is a true street BMX rider, bringing technical precision, creativity, and pure aggression to every spot he hits. Known for his fast, stylish riding and clean execution, he stands out in the Ukrainian BMX scene. Whether it’s technical rail combos or massive gaps, his riding speaks for itself.

On Instagram, he shares insane clips from the streets, while on TikTok (@notrikz), he stacks up views with heavy tricks and behind-the-scenes moments. With over 5,900 followers on IG and 216,000+ likes on TikTok, his presence in the scene keeps growing.

Rolling with top Ukrainian riders like Vadim Kononenko and Andrii Nesterov, he’s always pushing BMX culture forward. No unnecessary hype—just real street riding, heavy tricks, and the love for the game.

Kolya Sway

Kolya Sway is a skilled Ukrainian MTB rider known for his impressive street riding and technical tricks. Based in Kyiv, he actively shares his adventures and challenges on Instagram and YouTube, where he connects with the MTB community.

Kolya has participated in "Game of Bike" sessions, often riding alongside fellow enthusiast Bogdanus3331 in skateparks like Irpin. Through his engaging content, Kolya continues to inspire and motivate riders, contributing to the growth of the MTB culture in Ukraine.
